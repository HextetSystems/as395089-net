BGP Route Research

Do you know which paths you take to destinations that matter?
Are you aware when bogus paths are propagated to you?

Knowing which way your traffic is flowing on the internet can be tricky.

Hextet Systems has developed, and is running BGP route logging software to help build a better understanding of how various networks connect to each other.

Network Monitoring

Do you know how often your connection is maxed out or nearly maxed out?
Do you know how stable your latency is to your VoIP provider?
How long would a Denial of Service attack take to be noticed?

Knowing exactly what is happening on your network is a very critical aspect to running an Internet connected network. Hextet Systems is strongly committed to monitoring all aspects of network performance, including: latency/jitter, availability, primary/redundant path status, netflow, (un)authorized access.
